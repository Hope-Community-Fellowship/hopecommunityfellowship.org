What We Believe

We believe in these unchanging truths from the Bible

  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God
  • The Genesis account of creation in six literal days
  • The sanctity of human life
  • God eternally exists as Father, Son and Holy Spirit
  • God loved humanity so much that He gave his Son, Jesus, virgin born, to live as a man among us and die as a sacrifice for the sin of the world, to satisfy God’s righteous demand against sin.
  • Jesus was raised from the dead, is alive today, and will one day return to gather believers to live with Him forever
  • Jesus is the Savior of the world, and individuals are saved through personal faith in Him
  • It is the mission of the church to preach the gospel throughout the world
